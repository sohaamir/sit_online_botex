Adjusting your browser size


So the task is correctly proportioned, we ask that you change the size of your browser window beforehand if needed.

To make your browser window larger/smaller:

Mac: Press [⌘ and +] to zoom in, or [⌘ and -] to zoom out

Windows: Press [Ctrl and +] to zoom in, or [Ctrl and -] to zoom out


When you are ready, press the next button. You will be presented with a page where you can ensure that your browser is correctly proportioned, as you will not be able to scroll when completing the games (both practice and main game).


Once you have done so, please keep this browser size until the main task is completed.
