Practice Game Instructions


To get familiar with the task, you will now get to play 4 practice rounds with four other players.


The practice game is set up in the same way as the main game, but there are no switches.


This is just to get familiar with how the game works: which buttons to press and when.


The time provided for each of the individual sections is also longer in the practice game (approximately 6 seconds per choice), compared to the actual game (3 seconds per choice), so you can understand the different components and have more time to make your decisions.


The practice task itself will last approximately 4 minutes.


Please also change the size of your browser window during this practice session to match the page if needed.

You should be able to see the white boundaries around the page as demonstrated below:


Please ensure that these white boundaries are correctly proportioned to your screen, so you can see all of the images well.


When you are ready, press the next button when you are ready to play the practice game. We will put you into a waiting room where you will be matched with four other players.

It should take no longer than a minute or two to be matched (which is factored into your payment).
