Practice Game Instructions


Before you play the main game, you will now get to play 5 practice rounds. In this practice game, you will not be playing against the other players. The images representing the choices made by others in the group have been pre-determined.



This is just to get familiar with how the game works: which buttons to press and when.


The practice game is set up to work in the same way as the main game, but there are no switches and does not count towards your bonus payment.


The images themselves will also be different between the two games.


The time provided for each of the individual decisions is also longer (6 seconds per choice), compared to the main game (3 seconds per choice), so you can understand the different components and have more time to make your decisions.


But the choices and bets will still be made for you if you do not respond in the time provided.



The practice game itself will last approximately 3 minutes.


When you are ready, press the next button. You will be taken to a transition page which will then start the game for you.
