Welcome to the social learning task!




In this experiment you will be presented with choosing between two images, which have different probabilities of giving a reward.


You will be playing with four other players of the same gender, who will also make the same choices at the same time.

However, there is no competition or co-operation with other players, your aim is solely to maximise the amount of points that you score.

The experiment will be split into two parts: a practice game and the main task, where the practice game is a shortened version which will allow you to familiarise yourself with the task and how it works.

In total, the experiment will last around 50 minutes. You will then be able to complete a series of questionnaires, before receiving payment.


Please ensure that the browser window for the study covers your entire screen so the experiment runs smoothly.

We will now describe how the task works. Please press the next button when you are ready to move onto the task instructions.
