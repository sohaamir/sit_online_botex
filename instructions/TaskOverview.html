Welcome to the study!




In this experiment, you will make a series of choices between two images, one of which will reward you by giving you points, whilst the other will reduce your score.


You will be playing with four other players in real time, who will also make the same choices.

However, there is no competition or co-operation with other players, your aim is solely to maximise the amount of points that you score which will be converted into a bonus payment at the end of the experiment. The more points you score, the higher your bonus payment is!

The experiment will be split into two parts: a practice game and the main game, where the practice game is a shortened version which will allow you to familiarise yourself with the game and how it works.

In total, this first part of the study (instructions, practice game and main game) will last around 40-50 minutes. You will then be able to complete the second part of the study, a series of questionnaires which will take about 20 minutes, before receiving payment for both parts. Please only continue if you have 60-70 minutes available to complete both sections, as both are important for the study. You will have the opportunity to have a short break before the second part.


The next page will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how the task works and what you are expected to do.

Press the next button when you are ready to move onto the instructions.
