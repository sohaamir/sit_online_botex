First Choice, Bet and Preference Choices


At the beginning of each round, you will be presented with the screen below, where you will make your first choice for which image you think will give a reward:



The other two players in the group are represented by the avatars, with each group consisting of same-gender participants.


The position of the other players on the screen does not change throughout the experiment such that Player 1 is always on the left and Player 2 is always on the right.


You will have 2.5 seconds to make this first choice between the two images. This is done by either pressing the 'Left' or 'Right' keys on your keyboard. Importantly, you cannot change your choice once it is made. If you do not make a choice within this time limit, an image will be selected at random for you.


The chosen image is highlighted with a yellow box when you make your choice, or when it has been made for you.



You will then be asked to place a 'bet' as to how confident you are that the image selected is the image with the higher probability of reward. This is done by pressing either the '1', '2' or '3' button on your keyboard. You cannot change your bet once it is made.

Again, you have 2.5 seconds to make your bet. If you do not make a bet within this time, a random bet will be placed for you.

The chosen number is highlighted with a yellow box when the bet is made.



You will then be asked to choose one of the other two players choices to uncover first, by pressing either '1' or '2'. Pressing '1' will select the avatar on the left, pressing '2' will select the avatar on the right.

You then select the other player's choice to uncover second by pressing the remaining button. You will have 2 seconds to choose each, a random choice will be made for you if you do not make either choice within this time.


The avatar for the player selected as the 'first preference' is subsequently highlighted with a red box, whilst the avatar for the player selected as the 'second preference' is highlighted with an orange box.

The first preference is highlighted in red

Followed by the second preference in orange


The choices made by each player is then uncovered in turn below their avatar, starting with the player selected as the 'first preference', followed by the player selected as the 'second preference'.

Whilst you will see both players' choices eventually, it is important to carefully select the order in which you uncover the other players' choices based upon who's choice you value more.



Where players' do not make their first choice within the time-limit, the image displayed below their avatar choice will be dimmer. Therefore, you will know when players' choices were not made by the player themselves but were made by the computer.

In the example below, the player on the lefts' first choice was made by themselves, whilst it was made for them by the computer for the player on the right.
