First Choice, Bet and Preference Choices


At the beginning of each round, you will be presented with the screen below, where you will make your first choice for which image you think will give a reward.


The two images have different probabilities of giving a reward, with one being more likely to give a reward, and the other more likely to give a loss. Which image gives which is randomly determined at the start, but they will switch at certain points throughout the task.


The other four players in the group are represented by the avatars, with each group consisting of same-gender participants.


The position of the other players on the screen does not change throughout the experiment such that Player 1 is always on the far left followed by Players 2 and 3, and Player 4 on the far right.


You will have 2.5 seconds to make this first choice between the two images. This is done by either pressing the 'Left' or 'Right' keys on your keyboard. Importantly, you cannot change your choice once it is made. If you do not make a choice within this time limit, an image will be selected at random for you.


The chosen image is highlighted with a yellow box when you make your choice, or when it has been made for you.

After choosing the image on the left


You will then be asked to place a 'bet' as to how confident you are that the image selected is the image with the higher probability of reward. This is done by pressing either the '1', '2' or '3' button on your keyboard. You cannot change your bet once it is made.

Again, you have 2.5 seconds to make your bet. If you do not make a bet within this time, a random bet will be placed for you.

The chosen bet is highlighted with a yellow box when the bet is made.

Before making a bet

After making a bet of 2


You will then be asked to choose one of the other players choices to uncover first, by pressing either '1', '2', '3', or '4'. Pressing '1' will select Player 1, pressing '2' will select Player 2, '3' Player 3, and '4' Player 4. The avatar for the player selected as the 'first preference' is subsequently highlighted with a red box.


You then select another player's choice to uncover second by pressing either '1', '2', '3' or '4'. The avatar for this second player is highlighted with an orange box. You must select two different players and cannot choose the same player twice.

You will have 2 seconds to choose each, a random choice will be made for you if you do not make either choice within this time.

The choices made by each player is then uncovered in turn below their avatar, starting with the player selected as the 'first preference', followed by the player selected as the 'second preference'.

The choices for the two remaining players are then uncovered.


Whilst you will see all players' choices eventually, it is important to carefully select the order in which you uncover the other players' choices based upon who's choice you value more.



Where players do not make their first choice with in the time-limit, and are therefore made by the computer, the image displayed below their avatar choice will be dimmer.

In the example below, the choice for Player 1 and 2 was made by themselves, whilst it was made for Player 3 and 4 by the computer.
