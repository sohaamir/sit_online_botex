First Choice and Bet


At the beginning of each round, you will be presented with the screen below, where you will make your first choice for which image you think will give a reward.


The two images have different probabilities of giving a reward, with one being more likely to give a reward, and the other more likely to give a loss. Which image gives which is randomly determined at the start.


The other four players in the group are represented by avatars, towards the top of the screen.


The position of the other players on the screen does not change throughout the experiment such that Player 1 is always on the far left followed by Players 2 and 3, and Player 4 on the far right.


You will have 2.5 seconds to make this first choice between the two images. Importantly, you cannot change your choice once it is made. If you do not make a choice within this time limit, an image will be selected at random for you.

To make your choice, press either the left or right arrow key on your keyboard.


The chosen image is highlighted with a yellow box when you make your choice, or when it has been made for you.

You will then be asked to place a 'bet' as to how confident you are that the image selected is the image with the higher probability of reward. You cannot change your bet once it is made.


To make your bet, press either the '1', '2' or '3' key on your keyboard.


Again, you have 2.5 seconds to make your bet. If you do not make a bet within this time, a random bet will be placed for you.

The chosen bet is highlighted with a yellow box when the bet is made.

Before making a bet

After making a bet of 3


Your bonus payment will be calculated from your points total in the task, which is partly determined by your initial choice and bet. It is therefore important to make your initial choice and bet appropriately to maximise your bonus.


Specifically, you should answer based on how confident you are because the number of points is multiplied by the bet.


This means that if you bet high, you win more if you are correct, but lose more if you are incorrect.


After you have made your choice and bet, the choices of the other players in the group are revealed.



Where players do not make their first choice with in the time-limit, and are therefore made by the computer, the image displayed below their avatar choice will be dimmer.

In the example below, the choice for Player 1 and 2 was made by themselves, whilst it was made for Player 3 and 4 by the computer.
