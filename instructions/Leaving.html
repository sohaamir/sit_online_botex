Exiting the session


Please do not close or refresh the browser at any point during the experiment.

Doing so will cause the task to end for yourself and the other players in the group, with the data not being used.


In the case that another player in your group leaves during the task, the task will stop suddenly. If this happens, please do not refresh your browser. After about 10 seconds you will see the screen below where you will be re-directed to Prolific.



If this happens, you will be paid for your time accordingly and your submission rate will not be affected.


If you decide to participate, please try to complete the entire task!


We will now ask a number of questions to ensure that you correctly understand the task.

Please press the button below to proceed when you are ready.
