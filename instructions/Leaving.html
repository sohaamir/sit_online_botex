Exiting the session and browser settings


PLEASE DO NOT CLOSE OR REFRESH THE BROWSER AT ANY POINT DURING THE EXPERIMENT.

Doing so will un-synchronise the group, meaning that the data may not be used.


In the case that another player in your group leaves or disconnects during the game, it will continue to run, but their decisions will be made by a computer (the image will be more transparent).


If this happens, you will still be able to complete the study, you will be paid for your time accordingly and your submission rate will not be affected. However, the group's data may not be used.


Ultimately, if you decide to participate, please try to complete the entire experiment!



The task will run on commonly used browsers (e.g., Chrome, Safari, Firefox), but having other applications open may cause slight lag issues. To ensure the smoothness of the experiment and your user experience, please keep only the current tab open in your browser (i.e, close all other tabs and windows), and also close unused software and applications on your computer.
