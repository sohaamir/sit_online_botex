Welcome to the Moral Inference Task

In this experiment, you will learn about different agents and make decisions involving these agents.

The experiment has three phases for each agent:
Phase 1: Prior Beliefs

You'll report your initial impressions about an agent's moral character before learning about them.

Phase 2: Prediction Phase

You'll make predictions about the agent's choices in various scenarios. You'll receive feedback on your predictions and occasionally update your beliefs about the agent.

Phase 3: Trust Game

You'll play an economic game with the agent, deciding how much money to entrust to them.

Important: Your performance in the prediction phase will help you in the trust game, so pay attention to the feedback!

You will complete these three phases for each of three different agents.
