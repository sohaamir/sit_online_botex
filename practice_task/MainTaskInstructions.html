Main Task Instructions


You will now play the main game, where this time, you will play with four other people. This will last approximately 20 minutes, and consists of 60 rounds.

Pay attention, as the image more and less likely to give you points will now switch multiple times within the game.



The time provided for each of the individual choices is shorter compared to the practice game that you just completed (3 seconds compared to 6), so please provide your responses in time! If you do not, remember that a random choice will be made for you at the time limit, which may affect your score.

Please press the next button when you are ready to play the main game. You will be placed into a waiting room where it may a few minutes for the group to be formed as some players may complete the instructions and practice task quicker than others, so please be patient!



Please do your best to make your choices and bets manually; if you have come this far, we hope you are quite motivated!
