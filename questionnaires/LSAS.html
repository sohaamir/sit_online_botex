Questionnaire 1

This questionnaire assesses your feelings in various social situations. For each situation, please answer:

Question A: How anxious or fearful do you feel in this situation?
Choose from: None (0), Mild (1), Moderate (2), or Severe (3)

Question B: How often do you avoid this situation?
Choose from: Never (0%), Occasionally (1-33%), Often (34-66%), or Usually (67-100%)

Please answer both questions for each of the following situations:

Situation 1: Telephoning in public

How anxious or fearful do you feel when telephoning in public?

How often do you avoid telephoning in public?

Situation 2: Participating in small groups

How anxious or fearful do you feel when participating in small groups?

How often do you avoid participating in small groups?
