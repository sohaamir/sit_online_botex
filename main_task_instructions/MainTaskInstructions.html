Main Task Instructions


You will now play the main task. This will last around x minutes, and consists of 80 rounds.

Pay attention, as the image more and less likely to give a reward will now switch between the two at certain points within the task.


The time provided for each of the individual sections is shorter compared to the practice that you just completed (2-3 seconds compared to 8), so please remember to provide your responses in time! If you do not, remember that a random choice will be made for you at the time limit.

Please press the next button when you are ready to play the main game. You will be placed into another waiting room, and again it may take a minute or two for the group to be formed (which is factored into your payment).

The players in your group may be the same, or different from the players you just played with in the practice game. Again, you may choose to return to Prolific by pressing the button provided on the waiting page; you will still be paid for your time and your Prolific completion rate will be unaffected. However, you will miss out on the opportunity to win a bonus payment, and to take part in the study.


Although if you have come this far, we hope you are quite motivated to complete the task!
