Task Instructions

Welcome to the reversal learning task. In this task, you will make a series of choices between two options: Option A and Option B.

In each round, one of these options will be more likely to give you a reward than the other option. Your goal is to figure out which option is better and choose it to maximize your score.

Important: The better option can change during the task without warning. When this happens, the previously better option becomes the worse option, and vice versa. This is called a "reversal".

You will receive feedback after each choice to help you learn which option is currently better.

Your final score will be the sum of all points earned during the task. Try to do well, the higher your points score, the more your financial bonus payment is!

Good luck!
